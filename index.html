THE DATA BEHIND YOUR BEATS

We don't just listen. We analyze 8+ technical parameters for every track.

METRIC 01

VALENCE

A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness. High valence = Happy/Cheerful sounds.

METRIC 02

DANCEABILITY

Describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm stability, and beat strength.

METRIC 03

ACOUSTICNESS

A confidence measure of whether the track is acoustic. Helps identify your 'Deep Work' environment.

METRIC 04

ENERGY

Represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Crucial for detecting potential burnout levels.
